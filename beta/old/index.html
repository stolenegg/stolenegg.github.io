stolenegg is a small design & development consultancy based in Leeds, UK.
We create standards compliant web sites and bespoke software applications, as well as designing for print & new media.

Web Design:
 

Unlike many design agencies, we work to the latest W3C standards. XHTML & CSS throughout means beautiful, compliant & accessible sites.

We can also integrate Flash animations and applications for a media rich experience.

The web is changing - we'll ensure your site works and looks great for any user on any device.

Web Application & Portal Development:

From simple e-commerce and content management systems to full integration with your existing database, we can bring your business truly online.

PHP/MySQL is our platform of choice. We can also work in ASP for Microsoft platforms or Ruby on Rails for rapid application deployment.

Graphic Design for Digital & Print Media:

High quality and consistent branding, from document templates to business stationery, viral marketing & more.
